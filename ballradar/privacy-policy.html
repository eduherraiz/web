Privacy Policy

Ball Radar  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Ball Radar application handles user information. We take your privacy seriously: the app is designed to operate without collecting or sending any personal data to any server.

1. Data the app does NOT collect

Ball Radar does not collect, store or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • Your GPS location is never recorded or transmitted to any external server.
  • No user account is created.
  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
  • No advertisements are shown.
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone or any other device data.

2. Use of GPS

The app requests location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) solely to display your position on the radar in real time and calculate the distance to the game balls. This information is processed exclusively on your device and never leaves your phone.

3. Locally stored data

Ball Radar stores only the coordinates of balls defined by the user and the language preference in the device's local storage (SharedPreferences). This data is never synced to any external service and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

4. Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data (no Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK or similar services are used).

5. Children

Ball Radar does not collect information from any user, regardless of age. The app is suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated in the future, the new version will be published at this same URL with a revised update date.
